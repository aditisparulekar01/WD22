Veg Noodles

Ingredients:

Noodles,1 tsp Salt,1 tsp Oil,1 tsp Garlic paste,1 tsp Ginger paste,1/2 cup Beans,1/2 cup Cabbage, chopped,1/2 cup Carrot chopped ,1/2 cup Spring onion chopped,1/2 cup Capsicumchopped,2 tbsp Soy sauce.

Instructions:

  1. First rinse and shred or chop all the veggies, then set aside.
  2. You will need 1 cup shredded cabbage, ½ shredded carrots, 8 to 10 french beans (thinly sliced or finely chopped), ⅓ sliced capsicum and ⅓ cup chopped spring onion whites.
  3. Next heat 5 to 6 cups of water in a pan.
  4. Add salt after boiling and later add oil.Cook the noodles you will be using according to the package instructions as every variety of noodle will have a different cooking time.
  5. Then drain the cooked noodles in a colander or sieve.
  6. After that, set aside. You can even toss the noodles recipe with 2 to 3 teaspoons oil if desired.Now heat 2 to 3 tablespoons of toasted sesame oil in a wok or pan. Keep the heat to low or medium-low.
  7. Add 1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic and 1 teaspoon finely chopped ginger.
  8. Add veggies and You can also decrease the flame if the heat becomes too much to handle , then stir fry the veggies on a medium heat.
  9. Next add 1 to 2 tablespoons of soy sauce. Mix well and later add noodles.Add pepper and stir well.
  10. Finally, serve this veg noodles recipe steaming hot as it is or with some black bean sauce or tomato sauce.

Manchurian

Ingredients:

1/2 cup cabbage,1/2 cup carrot,1/2 cup capsicum,1/2 cup spring onion,1 tsp ginger,1 tsp garlic3 tbsp corn flour,2 tbsp maida,2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Take all the veggies, finely chopped, in a bowl along with ginger, garlic, corn flour, maida, salt, black pepper and soy sauce.
  2. Make round balls out of the thick vegetable mixture.
  3. Now deep fry the vegetable balls in a pan.
  4. Heat some oil in a pan and roast ginger, garlic and green chilli in it for a while
  5. Add spring onion, tomato ketchup, chilly sauce, soya sauce and vinegar followed by salt and black pepper.
  6. Mix thoroughly and put the fried vegetable balls into the mixture.
  7. Garnish with spring onion and celery. Serve hot along with cooked rice.
